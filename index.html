campus life

school library

Was Established in 1990

One day, it was our library period and and we were in our library reading books. Me and my friends had decided in the class only that we all will sit sit in the same row beside each and will have a talk while hiding our faces with the books. I was just holding the book in front of my face and our teacher came and asked me that, "Srajan are you reading the book ?" Since I was talking with my friends I didn't pay attention that I was holding the book upside-down and replied ,"Yes Mam" and then I realised that what I had done. After that we all were scolded by the teacher.
